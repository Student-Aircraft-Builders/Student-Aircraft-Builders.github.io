Project Goals

  • Complete the sheet metal assembly and have a working engine for the Zeinth Stol CH 750 by December 2015.
  • Select and purchase a composite plane and develop club infrastructure to train members in composite construction by August 2015.
  • Complete carbon fiber modifications to a glider by April 2015 and design and build a flying wing by December 2015.

Member Goals

  • Utilize club and university resources to provide members with professional development opportunities.
  • Ensure that members have consistent and meaningful access to practical hands-on experience.
  • Foster a community of supportive peers and encourage networking within SAB.

Club Goals

  • Retain active members and align our member experience to the needs of the students.
  • Define and implement club brand.
  • Maintain and promote a culture of responsibility among all members of SAB.
